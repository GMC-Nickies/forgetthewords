THE WAY OF THE SULTANATE

There are three tenets one must keep in walking the Plane:

1. Assert your will, keeping reverence only to the Holy One who is above us all. Even the Great Sultan is only a servant. But do not regard members of any sector as your peer, but at most as a student, and yourself the disciplinary teacher.

2. Follow the Rules of Engagement when dealing with other Planewalkers. Follow them for the sake of following them â€“ do not believe in them for yourself, for the time will come when they will be cast aside.

3. The Plane can be horrifying to the soul. Careful that you do not tread too lightly, lest you be consumed. For in much wisdom is much grief: and he that increaseth knowledge increaseth sorrow. Yet the purpose of life is not to be happy.
